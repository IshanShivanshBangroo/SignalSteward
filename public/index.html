Signal, Edit, Support

A short chat activity about useful public updates, crowd coordination, and support networks.

Developed by Ishan • Designed by Nicole

What it does: turns three short responses into a public stance card, a visible class wall, and two balanced debate teams.

What you will do

  1. Enter your name
  2. Chat for three short turns
  3. Get a stance score and public card
  4. Wait for balanced debate teams

Activity view

How the three readings work here
  • Signal lens — useful short updates improve situational awareness when they tell people where, what changed, and what to do.
  • Edit lens — a crowd improves a shared page only when coordination keeps quality from turning into noise.
  • Support lens — different ties provide different kinds of help, reach, trust, and visibility.
